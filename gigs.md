I started DJing in May 2017 and regularly played <a href="https://vimeo.com/463652373" target="_blank">themed Tea Time tunes</a> at Twitter HQ. I usually DJed with <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/juliengaillard1/" target="_blank">Julien Gaillard</a> on the <a href="https://vimeo.com/463687716" target="_blank">guitar</a>. Since June 2019, I've been the tour DJ for <a href="https://soundcloud.com/ohsimsimah" target="_blank">Sim Simah</a> and <a href="https://music.apple.com/us/artist/aarxn-cxle/1453179768" target="_blank">Aarxn Cxle</a> for a few of their shows. The picture above is by <a href="https://www.instagram.com/meria.agency/?hl=ne" target="_blank">Jesse Meria</a> from July 2019 at <a href="https://www.thenewparish.com/" target="_blank">The New Parish</a>, where we were <a href="https://www.teamllfent.com/news/2019/7/30/camron-with-special-guest-troyllf" target="_blank">the opening act</a>.
